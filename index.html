About me

I was born and lived in Athens; However, recently, I moved in Mytilene, Lesvos, in order to continue my studies on 3D Design and Animation as a PHD Candidate on Department of Cultural Technology and Communication at University of Aegean. Currently I am a post graduated student in (M.Sc.) Interested in 3D Design and animation as an content creator. Also occupied with Digital Culture, VR and AR Development , Mobile App and Web Design and Development.

3D Design

In recent years, I have been involved in 3D Design, starting as a hobby. I have now collaborated with various stores, to create posters, and lighting posters. In the past I had designed 3D objects for the implementation of my master's thesis which either concerned the imprinting of 2D images in 3D, or original designs that were first designed by hand.

Animation Design

I 've started 3D animation as a hobby in 2019 making a spot for the pride event. Then I created animations for my master's thesis which were displayed on an AR mobile application. Then I realized some collaborations by making commercials for small private companies, as well as videos that were projected, complementing the corresponding music.
